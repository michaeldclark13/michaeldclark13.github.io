Unity Cloth Simulation

This cloth simulator features a red sphere that can be used to interact with the cloth. Simply drag the sphere to come in contact with the cloth to simulate a proper physics response. The camera angle can be changed by dragging the mouse along the background of the view window. Enjoy!
